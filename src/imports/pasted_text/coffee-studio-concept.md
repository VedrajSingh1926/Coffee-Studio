PROJECT NAME: COFFEE STUDIO

TAGLINE:
From Bean to Emotion

PROJECT TYPE:
Premium Interactive Frontend Experience Website

GOAL:
Design a visually stunning, modern, dark-themed storytelling website that uses coffee as a metaphor for creativity, ambition, ideas, emotions, and personal growth. The website should feel like a luxury digital experience rather than a coffee shop or ecommerce website.

STYLE:

* Premium
* Cinematic
* Minimal yet immersive
* Apple-style polish
* Luxury coffee aesthetics
* Interactive storytelling
* Modern glassmorphism
* Soft glowing effects
* High-end portfolio quality

COLOR PALETTE:
Background: #0A0A0A
Cream: #F5F1E8
Coffee Brown: #6F4E37
Gold Accent: #C9A66B
Smoke White: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

TYPOGRAPHY:
Headings:
Elegant serif typography

Body:
Clean modern sans-serif

GENERAL DESIGN RULES:

* Full-screen sections
* Smooth transitions
* Glassmorphism panels
* Floating particles
* White drifting smoke
* Layered depth
* Soft shadows
* Premium hover animations
* No ecommerce elements
* No pricing cards
* No product listings
* No shopping experience
